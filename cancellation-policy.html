Cancellation Policy

The initial booking amount is non-refundable in the event of any cancellation.

  • For cancellations made 30 days or more before the trip's start date:
    50% of the total trip cost will be retained as cancellation fees.
  • For cancellations made 15–30 days before the trip's start date:
    75% of the total trip cost will be charged as cancellation fees.
  • For cancellations made within 0–15 days of the trip's start date:
    100% of the total trip cost will be charged as cancellation fees.
Unforeseen circumstances:
In the event of weather conditions or government restrictions, certain activities may be canceled. While alternative activities may be offered, no refunds will be provided in such cases.
