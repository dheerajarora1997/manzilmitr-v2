Jibhi Tirthan

Escape to the quiet charm of Jibhi and Tirthan, where wooden cottages, hidden waterfalls, and emerald forests create a dreamlike retreat. Wander through misty trails, relax beside crystal-clear rivers, and wake up to birdsong and mountain views. Surrounded by untouched Himalayan landscapes, this serene escape offers slow travel, soulful moments, and a deep connection with nature and peaceful hill life.

Itinerary

Day 0: Departure to Jibhi

Assemble at pickup point in the evening. Trip leader will give small briefing about the trip followed by Introduction of the group. halt for dinner (own expenses).

Day 1: Jibhi

Reach Jibhi, check-in at hotel and freshen up. Explore Jibhi Waterfall and Mini Thailand. Enjoy the beautiful view of the valley and spend some time at the riverside. Reach back to hotel and enjoy Bonfire and Music followed by dinner and overnight stay at the hotel

Day 2: Jalori Pass

Breakfast will be served at the hotel. After Breakfast leave for Jalori Pass. Start your trek to Serolsar lake(4-5 Km) while enjoying 360 degree viewpoint in between. After coming back from the trek, have dinner and overnight stay at hotel.

Day 3: Tirthan Valley

After Breakfast, check-out from the hotel. Depart for Tirthan Valley and Choiee Waterfall. After coming back enjoy the mesmerizing view of Tirthan valley. After Tirthan Valley exploration, head back to Delhi
