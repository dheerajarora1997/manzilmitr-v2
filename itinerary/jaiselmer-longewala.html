Jaiselmer Longewala

Step into the golden sands of Jaisalmer and the historic grounds of Longewala. Explore ornate havelis, ancient forts, and vibrant bazaars, then journey into the Thar Desert to witness tales of bravery at Longewala. Camel rides, desert sunsets, and star-lit skies blend history, adventure, and timeless desert magic into an unforgettable Rajasthani escape.

Itinerary

Day 0: Departure to Jaiselmer

Assemble at pickup point in the evening. Trip leader will give small briefing about the trip followed by Introduction of the group. halt for dinner (own expenses).

Day 1: Jaiselmer

Reach Jaisalmer, check-in to swiss camps at Sam sand dunes and freshen up. Get around the beautiful desert in the evening. Enjoy sunset Camel safari. Enjoy traditional Rajasthani folk music and dance program around the bonefire and light snacks. Dinner and overnight stay at the camps.

Day 2: Longewala Battle Field

Bed tea will be served at the camps. Go for Jeep safari and experience beautiful sunrise at Thar Desert. Have breakfast at the resort and leave for sightseeing at Longewala war memorial (famous for the Indo-Pak battle in 1971). After this, visit Tanot Mata Mandir and head towards the resort. Enjoy the Traditional Rajasthani music and dance program around the bonfire and DJ party at the resort. Dinner and overnight stay in camps.

Day 3: Jaiselmer City Tour

Wake up early, freshen up and have breakfast. Leave for Jaisalmer city tour. En route, visit kuldhara the haunted village. Explore Gadisar Lake, Jaisalmer Fort and Patwon ki Haveli and enjoy an evening at Jaisalmer flea market for shopping and local food. Board the bus to Delhi and reach Delhi by next morning.
