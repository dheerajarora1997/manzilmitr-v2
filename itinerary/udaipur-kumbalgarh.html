Udaipur Kumbalgarh

Discover the royal charm of Udaipur and Kumbhalgarh, where timeless palaces, serene lakes, and mighty forts tell stories of Rajasthan’s regal past. Wander through Udaipur’s romantic streets, witness golden sunsets over Lake Pichola, and explore the majestic walls of Kumbhalgarh—an elegant journey blending heritage, history, and old-world grandeur.

Itinerary

Day 0: Departure to Udaipur

Group will assemble at the pickup point. Trip leader will give small briefing about the trip, followed by Introduction of the group. Halt for dinner (not on us).

Day 1: Udaipur

Reach Udaipur, check-in at the hotel and freshen up. Leave for Udaipur local sightseeing. Visit Saheliyon ki Bari, Fateh Sagar Lake, Under the sun Aquarium and Maharana Pratap Memorial. Enjoy evening at Fateh sagar market area for a beautiful sunset and local street food. Reach back to hotel, have dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 2: Udaipur

Breakfast will be served at the hotel. Explore Lake Pichola, City Palace, Jagdish Temple, Karni Mata ropeway and famous Gangaur Ghat. Evening can be spent either by enjoying culture and folk art performance at Bagore Ki Haveli(7-8 pm) or by exploring local flea market and lake side cafes. Dinner and overnight stay at hotel.

Day 3: Kumbalgarh

After Breakfast, check-out from the hotel. Depart for famous Kumbalgarh fort. Kumbalgarh fort is one of the most beautiful forts in Rajasthan and is recognised as a UNESCO World Heritage Site with second longest wall in the world. After exploring Kumbalgarh by evening, board the bus to Delhi.
