Mcleodganj Bir Billing

Experience the soulful vibes of McLeodganj and the thrill of Bir Billing. Immerse yourself in Tibetan culture, monasteries, and hillside cafés, then soar above lush valleys with world-class paragliding in Bir. Framed by dramatic Himalayan landscapes, this journey blends spirituality, adventure, and free-spirited mountain energy into an unforgettable escape.

Itinerary

Day 0: Departure to Mcleodganj

Assemble at pickup point in the evening. Trip leader will give small briefing about the trip followed by Introduction of the group. halt for dinner (own expenses).

Day 1: Mcleodganj

Reach Mcloedganj, check-in at hotel and freshen up. Leave for Mcloaedganj flea market. Explore Bhagsu waterfall, Dalai Lama Monastery and local cafes. Reach back to hotel and enjoy Bonfire and Music followed by dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 2: Mcleodganj to Bir

Breakfast will be served at the hotel. After Breakfast leave for Bir. En route, we will explore Dharamshala HPCA Stadium, Palampur tea gardens and Baijnath Temple. After reaching Bir in the evening, check-in and freshen-up. Enjoy the Bonfire with music. Dinner and overnight stay at hotel.

Day 3: Bir Billing

After Breakfast, check-out from the hotel. Leave for paragliding at Paragliding take-off sight at Bir Billing (extra charges for Paragliding). Take-off in Billing and Land in Bir in next 15-30 minutes. Enjoy the beautiful views while this daring adventure. After exploring Bir, board the bus to Delhi.
